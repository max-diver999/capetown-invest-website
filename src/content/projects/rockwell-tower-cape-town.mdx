## Cape Town Invest lens on The Rockwell

Searchers typing rockwell tower cape town usually mean The Rockwell — not a separate tower brand on the Foreshore. The building is a De Waterkant landmark: brick-and-steel architecture inspired by early Manhattan lofts, an internal courtyard with floating walkways, and ground-floor restaurants on a pedestrianised street segment absorbed into the scheme. Styleprops launched around 2009, selling roughly half the apartments to private owners before operating remaining units as the Rockwell All-Suite Hotel and Apartments.

That hybrid history matters for diligence. Some doors trade freely on the secondary market; others sit in hotel rental pools with management agreements and owner-use calendars. De Waterkant sits between the [V&A Waterfront](/guides/cape-town-property-investment-guide/) leisure corridor and the [City Bowl](/guides/cape-town-city-bowl-property-investment/) grid — walkable to Cape Quarter, Bree Street nightlife, and Green Point stadium events.

## Building layout and unit types

MWP's design stacks ground-floor retail and restaurants beneath six residential levels and a penthouse crown, with units accessed via walkways across an enclosed courtyard — a typology that keeps weather protection for De Waterkant's windy season. Formats span one-bedroom studios through four-bedroom penthouses with city, mountain, and harbour aspects.

Booking.com and agency listings cite two-bedroom apartments around 81 m² with open kitchens and balconies facing Table Mountain — useful size anchors when you rebuild price per square metre on resale. Penthouses loft-style layouts target owner-occupiers and premium short-stay guests.

## Hotel pool versus pure sectional title

Before the 2010 World Cup, Styleprops reportedly placed unsold inventory into a boutique hotel operation while continuing to sell individual apartments — creating a dual market inside one body corporate. Hotel-pooled units may carry:

- Minimum rental participation clauses.
- Owner-use blackout dates.
- Operator fees of 15% to 25% on gross bookings.
- Furnished fit-out obligations.

Private resale units outside the pool behave like standard sectional title with self-managed long-let or City-regulated short-stay — subject to conduct rules. Read the schedule of conditions and management agreement before you assume Airbnb freedom.

| Ownership mode | Best for | DD focus |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Private sectional title | Long-let, owner-use | Levies, conduct rules |
| Hotel pool | Hands-off income | Revenue share, calendar |
| Penthouse | Trophy + short-stay | Body corporate premium |

## Location and tenant demand

De Waterkant is the colourful Georgian-style grid between Green Point and the CBD — popular with LGBT-friendly nightlife, design studios, and Waterfront workers who want a village feel. The Rockwell's Prestwich address places tenants within minutes of Cape Quarter lifestyle retail and the Waterfront ferry.

Tenant depth combines tourism (short-stay), creative industry (long-let), and semigration professionals. Wind-sheltered courtyard design is a real amenity — De Waterkant terraces that face north without protection can sit empty in summer wind weeks.

## Foreign buyer workflow on resale

1. Confirm unit is not encumbered by hotel pool unless you want that model.
2. Offer with suspensive bond; finance up to about 50% as non-resident.
3. FICA and transfer duty on resale via conveyancer.
4. Review two years body corporate financials and special levies.
5. Non-resident endorsement for repatriation.
