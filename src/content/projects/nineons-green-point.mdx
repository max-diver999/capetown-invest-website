## Foreign buyer workflow on resale

Non-residents buy NINEONS like any sectional title resale:

1. Offer on live listing with suspensive bond clause if financing locally.
2. FICA through conveyancer; funds via authorised dealer.
3. Transfer duty on resale (no VAT on secondary sales).
4. Non-resident endorsement on title for repatriation.
5. Bond up to about 50% LTV for qualifying non-residents.

Full steps: [foreign buyer hub](/guides/buy-cape-town-property-foreigner/) and [cost of buying](/guides/cost-of-buying-property-cape-town/).
