## Fluent management and investor economics

Fluent markets THREE43ONB as work-from-anywhere ready with high-speed WiFi, dedicated workstations, and serviced stays for guests and owners. For owners, the relevant documents are the management contract, house rules on owner occupation, revenue allocation, and maintenance responsibilities. Aparthotel models often cap owner nights or route all bookings through the operator, which can clash with buyers who expect unrestricted personal use.

Build three scenarios: long-let annual lease, Fluent-managed short-stay with 55% to 65% occupancy, and blended use. Only the long-let scenario should gate your purchase decision unless you explicitly want a hospitality asset.

## Location on Main Road Sea Point

Sea Point combines promenade running, Atlantic beaches, and dense restaurant supply. THREE43ONB's corner visibility supports brand recognition for Fluent bookings, which matters for occupancy. It also exposes the building to traffic and nightlife noise on lower floors, so floor and aspect selection affects both rent and resale.

MyCiTi bus routes along Main Road support car-light tenants, a growing preference among relocating professionals. Compare against De Waterkant walkability at ONEONR if you want CBD proximity instead of promenade lifestyle.

## Pros, cons, and buyer fit

**Pros**

- Completed operating stock with observable management performance.
- Blok design and Fluent operations reduce remote-owner friction.
- Rooftop pool, concierge, and retail support guest ratings and ADR.
- Walkable Atlantic Seaboard address with no foreign buyer surcharge.
- Generator backup for communal areas mitigates load-shedding complaints.

**Cons**

- Management fees compress net yield versus unmanaged long-let.
- Sea Point rates and levies run above inland income precincts.
- Short-stay regulation and body corporate rules can change.
- Parking and paid amenities add tenant and owner cost.
- Hospitality assets can be harder to finance than plain residential.

## Serviced stay compliance checklist

Before you model short-stay income at THREE43ONB, confirm the sectional title conduct rules, the City of Cape Town guest accommodation by-laws applicable to your unit, and whether Fluent requires exclusive booking management. Document insurance for contents and public liability if you let furnished. Read the [Airbnb investment Cape Town guide](/guides/airbnb-investment-cape-town-guide/) for regulation context, then stress-test a long-let fallback at MODELED 4.5% to 6% gross so the deal still works if hospitality rules tighten.

Corner buildings on Main Road also face asymmetric light and wind exposure: units with Bellevue Road frontage may trade differently from internal courtyard stacks with less road noise but reduced ocean glimpse. Inspect at different times of day before you lock aspect assumptions into a five-year model, and ask Fluent for historical occupancy by unit type if you are buying from a resale owner rather than primary release.
