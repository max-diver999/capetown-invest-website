## Cape Town Invest lens on The Ridge

Portfolio listings titled The Ridge Clifton usually lack a registered sectional-title scheme behind the name. On the ground, **The Ridge** is a place — the elevated Ridge Road corridor above Fourth Beach where Clifton's most private villas command panoramic Atlantic views and walk-to-sand access within minutes. New World Wealth research has ranked The Ridge among the most expensive streets in South Africa at roughly R96,000 per square metre, ahead of many V&A Marina berths and Bantry Bay cliff roads.

Investors Googling the ridge clifton may also land on **Clifton Terraces**, a completed SAOTA-designed terraced apartment building on Victoria Road (2018, about ten exclusive units), or on **Azalea Signature Residences**, a proposed about ten-apartment scheme at 13 Victoria Road with environmental authorisation in November 2024 but municipal planning still pending after resident objections. Cape Town Invest uses slug `the-ridge-clifton` to separate **street-level trophy stock** from those distinct products — and to warn against aggregator POA spam that invents Florida-registered sellers.

This is rarely a yield purchase. Foreigners at a large share of national sales above R10 million compete for Atlantic Seaboard scarcity with no buyer surcharge — read the [Atlantic Seaboard guide](/guides/atlantic-seaboard-property-investment-guide/) and [Clifton area guide](/areas/clifton-property-investment/) before you model rent on a R80 million villa.

## Clifton Terraces: the completed apartment anchor

Clifton Terraces by SAOTA delivered about ten exclusive apartments across stepped terraced levels on Victoria Road, completed around 2018 with a SAPOA award for innovative residential design. The building consolidates previously separated sites into a sculpted cliffside form with private terraces, cross-ventilation, and ocean views toward the Clifton beaches.

For buyers who want **apartment sectional title** with Clifton branding rather than a R80 million villa, Clifton Terraces is the verified completed scheme to compare — not The Ridge as a monolithic project. Resale underwriting runs on body corporate levies, conduct rules, and Atlantic Seaboard rental comparables from our [Clifton guide](/areas/clifton-property-investment/).

## Azalea and future supply at 13 Victoria Road

Environmental authorisation for a proposed signature apartment building at Erven 46 and 47 (13 Victoria Road) was issued in November 2024, with marketing describing about ten large-format terraced apartments, three basement levels, and 16 parking bays on a combined about 1,479 m² site. Press reporting in early 2025 noted resident objections and that the application had not yet reached the Municipal Planning Tribunal — treat shovel-ready auction marketing as **planning-risk stock** until a final approval and developer SPV are confirmed.

Future supply at this scale can affect resale liquidity for existing Clifton Terraces owners but also reinforces Clifton's scarcity narrative if approvals stay constrained.

## Villa stock on Ridge Road

Individual villas on Ridge Road and adjacent Clifton streets trade as freehold trophy assets — often Stefan Antoni or comparable contemporary architecture, rim-flow pools, elevator access, and cinema or gym wings. Listings appear on luxury rental portals as much as on sale mandates; a property marketed at R120,000 per night short-stay is not comparable to a long-let yield model.

## Foreign buyer workflow

Non-residents acquiring Clifton trophy stock follow standard freehold or sectional title paths:

1. Offer on verified mandate with suspensive bond if financing locally.
2. FICA and source-of-funds through conveyancer; offshore capital via authorised dealer.
3. Transfer duty on resale per SARS; no foreign buyer surcharge.
4. Non-resident endorsement on title for repatriation.
5. On sectional title (Clifton Terraces), review body corporate rules and levies.

Full steps: [foreign buyer hub](/guides/buy-cape-town-property-foreigner/) and [cost of buying](/guides/cost-of-buying-property-cape-town/).
