## 2 On Hill: EDGE beachfront off-plan

2 On Hill sits one road from Blouberg beach, marketed as 150 metres from the sand with two-bedroom apartments, patios, and built-in braais. Explore Developments positions the scheme as EDGE certified with basement parking on selected units.

Pricing runs R2.695 million to R11.75 million on published materials, including VAT with no transfer duty or transfer fees on primary sales — a meaningful cash-flow difference versus resale where duty applies. Marketing highlights Airbnb-ready layouts; verify body corporate conduct rules before you bank on short-stay income.

For investors comparing [Century City](/guides/century-city-property-investment-guide/) income stock, 2 On Hill trades lifestyle and seasonal letting for lower pure long-let yield certainty.

## Beiramar: fourteen units on Coral Road

Beiramar limits supply to 14 units on Coral Road with two- and three-bedroom layouts from about R2.9 million to R2.98 million, 60 to 80 m² interiors, and balconies up to 20 m². Lift access and inverter backup address load-shedding friction — a real tenant issue in coastal towers.

The site sits one street from Blouberg Beach, near Eden on the Bay and Table Bay Mall. It competes visually with Infinity Blouberg Beach hospitality branding on the same coast — differentiate on sectional title ownership versus hotel-tinged letting.

Eden on the Bay anchors retail and dining for the Blouberg strand, supporting walkable tenant amenity for Coral Road owners. ## Yield and letting strategy

The [Blouberg area guide](/areas/blouberg-property-investment/) models 6.5% gross and 4.5% net on family stock — stronger than many Atlantic Seaboard nodes at far lower entry. Beachfront off-plan marketing often quotes short-stay gross in peak season; rebuild net with:

- 11 months of rent in year one (December void risk).
- 20 to 25% management fee on short-stay if you use an operator.
- Levy inflation at 6% per year on new builds with backup power.
- Rates on coastal municipal accounts.

Compare against [Atlantic Seaboard guide](/guides/atlantic-seaboard-property-investment-guide/) if capital preservation beats yield.

## Transfer duty and VAT on off-plan

Primary sales from developers often include VAT in the price, avoiding transfer duty — verify the OTP and tax invoice. Resale on Coral Road corridors triggers duty on the SARS sliding scale. Foreigners still face 50% LTV ceilings; model the full stack in the [cost of buying guide](/guides/cost-of-buying-property-cape-town/).

## Seasonal cash-flow model for short-stay

Month-by-month budgeting beats agent peak-season gross quotes. Model January and December at 50% occupancy, February through November at 75% if body corporate permits short-stay, management fee at 20% of gross, and levy inflation at 6%. If net still beats Century City long-let on the same capital, short-stay thesis holds; if not, buy income inland.

Load-shedding resilience matters on the coast: Beiramar markets inverters; confirm battery replacement reserves in body corporate forecasts so special levies do not surprise you in year three.

Link off-plan diligence to [Infinity Milnerton](/projects/infinity-milnerton/) for Coral Road resale context and to [Milnerton](/areas/milnerton-property-investment/) if your tenant commutes to Century City rather than chasing peak-week beach rents.

Azul Terrace and other Marine Drive luxury launches price from about R4.75 million upward — a different buyer than 2 On Hill's sub-R3 million entry. Segment your comparables by ticket band before you average yields across the whole Blouberg coast.

Semigration families often choose Blouberg for schools and beach weekends while keeping offices in Century City or the CBD. That dual-location tenant supports long-let furnished contracts at 11-month terms — model that before you assume full summer short-stay seasonality on every beachfront OTP.

When agents cite Two Oceans Beach pricing, ask for the Mouille Point address and levy schedule — not a Blouberg off-plan brochure. Name confusion is the most common underwriting error on this corridor.
