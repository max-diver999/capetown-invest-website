## Heritage rebuild and aparthotel positioning

Prospekt retained the century-old facade while rebuilding interiors with herringbone floors, bespoke kitchens, and concierge security. Marketing positioned the scheme as aparthotel-ready — furnished resales now advertise Airbnb-ready studios with gym and coffee shop access.

Boutique scale matters: 36 units means body corporate decisions move faster than 200-unit towers, but levy per door can run high when concierge and commercial ground-floor services are shared across few owners.

---

## UDZ tax mechanics (South African buyers)

Venice House falls inside Cape Town's Urban Development Zone. South African taxpayers may claim accelerated depreciation on qualifying building costs — potentially material to after-tax cash flow in early ownership years. Building-versus-land split, purchase date, and current SARS schedules determine the allowance.

Foreign buyers with no South African taxable income should underwrite **zero UDZ benefit** unless a tax adviser structures otherwise. Do not pay Charlotte or Venice premiums solely for UDZ without a signed tax opinion.

## Short-stay versus long-let underwriting

City Bowl tourism recovered strongly post-Covid, supporting short-stay on Burg Street. Model net, not gross:

- Management fee near 15% to 20% on short-stay.
- Levies and rates on boutique buildings with concierge.
- November and winter void risk even in tourism nodes.
- Body corporate conduct rules on night noise and guest caps.

Long-let professional tenants offer lower gross but steadier net — compare against [CBD rental pipeline](/projects/chestercourt-redevelopment/) supply risk from Golden Acre studios near R10,000 per month.

## UDZ depreciation sketch for SA taxpayers

Urban Development Zone allowance accelerates depreciation on the building portion of purchase price for qualifying buyers. A R2.5 million studio with 70% building allocation might yield material first-year deductions for active SA taxpayers — foreign non-residents typically capture none.

Run the split with a tax practitioner before you pay a UDZ premium on asking price. The tax benefit is not transferable to the next owner in full; resale pricing may already capitalise partial UDZ value.

## Noise, security, and Friday-night DD

Burg Street sits one block from Long Street nightlife. Friday and Saturday noise is predictable, not exceptional — disclose to short-stay guests or price long-let tenants accordingly. Security is estate-grade for CBD: access control, CCTV, and concierge patterns similar to Charlotte.

Walk the block at 23:00 on a Saturday before you unconditional an offer. If silence is non-negotiable, compare [Foreshore Place](/projects/foreshore-place-cape-town/) professional tenant profile instead.

## Prospekt handover and defects window

Venice House targeted Q4 2024 completion after an October 2023 sell-out launch. Resale buyers inherit any latent defects only if prior owners failed to log them with the body corporate before transfer. Request snag lists from the seller, plumber pressure tests, and elevator service records in the first seven days after registration completes.
